Andrew Strong- Owner and Senior Technician

Andrew heads Strong Technology with a BA in Business Management and over 20 years of experience in the IT field. Starting his career as a computer technician for the Community Colleges of Spokane, Andrew went on to create Strong Technology Consulting in 2001. With an expertise in network engineering and security analysis, Andrew provides our clients with technology consulting and business advice.

Andrew’s philosophy is to treat each client’s business as if it is his own, providing the highest level of customer service and Business IT consulting.

Linda Strong- Co-Owner and Business Manager

Linda started out in education, earning an MA in History and working as both a teacher and museum educator, but has spent much of her professional career managing business offices. As Strong Technology’s business manager, she is in charge of bookkeeping, payables and receivables, payroll, and all other aspects of keeping STC running smoothly and efficiently.

In addition to her work in the office, Linda has also developed an extensive knowledge of HIPAA rules and regulations, serving as STC’s chief HIPAA Security officer. Most recently, Linda became certified as a HIPAA Security Professional, enabling her to complete HIPAA risk analyses and train others in HIPAA compliance best practices.

Tracy Strong- Junior Technician

Tracy comes to Strong Technology with over 20 years of experience in retail sales and merchandising management. As a junior technician, Tracy preps computers, orders and tracks STC merchandise, and serves as out shipping and receiving manager.

Amanda Strong- Social Media and Web Design Specialist
