Andrew Strong

Andrew is both the owner of the company and is the head Senior Technician. Andrew has a BA in Business Management and has worked in the IT field since 1994. Andrew started his career as a computer technician for the Community Colleges of Spokane. He worked for the Community Colleges until he started Strong Technology Consulting in 2001. With over twenty years of experience in the IT field, Andrew provides a high level of computer consulting for STC clients. Along with network engineering, security analysis, and technology expertise, Andrew provides our clients technology consulting and business advice. His philosophy is to treat each client’s business as if it is his own, providing the highest level of customer service and business IT consulting.

Linda Strong

Linda Strong has an MA in History. She started her career as a teacher and museum educator, but has spent most of her career managing business offices. Linda is Strong Technology’s co-owner and business manager. She is in charge of bookkeeping, payables and receivables, payroll, and all other aspects of keeping STC running smoothly. Linda also serves as STC’s HIPAA Security officer. Linda has extensive knowledge of HIPAA rules and regulations and she ensures STC is HIPAA compliant. Recently, she has her education experience to teach HIPAA compliance measures to STC medical offices.
