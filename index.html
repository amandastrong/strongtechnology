Founded in 2001 by Andrew and Linda Strong, Strong Technology Consulting, Inc. is a local, family owned business providing quality, full service IT support to professional offices both large and small throughout Eastern Washington and Northern Idaho. As your IT support provider, STC can either work with your existing IT department or act as your primary IT support, working towards stabilizing your infrastructure, network, servers, and computers. With our highly trained staff and years of experience working in a variety of industries, from health care and education, to legal and manufacturing, STC can meet all of your IT support needs through fast and efficient on-site, secure remote, or telephone support.

At STC, we believe creating a solid IT foundation for your business can increase your productivity and profitability. By acting proactively we try to prevent small problems from snowballing into big problems. We understand business, not just technology. STC will always give you options tailored to your needs, working around your specific goals, expectations, and budget.

Is your Business HIPAA Compliant?

As a HIPAA Compliant Business Associate, Strong Technology Consulting, Inc. can assist you in becoming and remaining HIPAA compliant by helping you create Security Policies, Best Practices, and Procedures, providing comprehensive training for your staff, conducting your periodic Security Risk Assessment, and connecting you with HIPAA friendly products.
