Strong Technology Consulting, Inc. was founded in 2001 by Andrew and Linda Strong. The focus of STC is providing quality IT Support and consulting for professional offices throughout Eastern Washington and Northern Idaho. Our clientele includes dental practices, vision centers, law firms, education institutions, and construction companies. Because many of our clients are medical practices, Strong Technology is HIPAA compliant and provides ongoing HIPAA Security Risk audits for their clients. As of now there are four highly trained, experienced professionals working for STC. The company is currently growing and plans to add more IT Professionals as needed.
